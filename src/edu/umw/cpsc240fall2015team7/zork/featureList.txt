Combat (25 xp) - Allows the Player to fight NPC's in the Dungeon. When the Player is in the same room as an NPC, he/she will have the opportunity to fight
with them in a turn-based system. Each NPC has health and a power level, which will determine how much damage will be dealt to the Player when the NPC attacks, and vice versa. Weapons (Melee and Guns(which require ammo, separate Items)) will be available to the Player to deal more damage to enemies.
