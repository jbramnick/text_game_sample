Welcome to Zork
Authors: Nathan, Jim, Carson

Basic commands:
	i- This will print out a list of everything in your inventory.
	take - This will take an item. Ex: take feather
	u,d,n,s,e,w - Will move in that direction
	help - Will print this helpful message.
	drop - Will drop the item named. Ex: drop feather.
	attack - Will attack the named npc with a melee weapon. If you do not have a weapon named it will pick one for you. Ex: attack zombie with sword
	shoot - Will attack the named npc with a gun. If you do not name a weapon it will pick one for you. Ex: shoot zombie with shotgun 
	look - Will print out the description of the room that you are in.
	save - Saves the game.
